> Our makeup training program is designed to help aspiring makeup artists develop the necessary skills and knowledge to become successful professionals in the beauty industry. We offer two programs: the Makeup Stylist program and the Qualified Makeup Artist program.

REGISTER TODAY

Makeup Stylist Program



-> Duration - 10 days (4 hours per day min)
-> Flexible timing (Daytime | Weekends)
-> Course Price: INR 25000
-> Location: SAHANA mehndi makeup nail studio academy

The Makeup Stylist program is a 10-day training course that is suitable for beginners with no prior experience. During this program, you will learn the basics of makeup artistry, including material science, type analysis, face measurement, professional priming, contouring, lifting with colour, professional eyebrow styling, type-appropriate eye shapes and shading techniques, lash line compression, eyelash bonding, and lip enhancement techniques. In addition, you will learn how to create different looks, such as natural, daytime, and evening makeup.

After completing the Makeup Stylist program, you can move on to the Qualified Makeup Artist program, which is a 15-day training course. This program is designed for those who want to take their skills to the next level and become professional makeup artists. The prerequisite for this program is the completion of our Makeup Stylist program.

Qualified Makeup Artist Program



-> Duration - 20 days (4 hours per day min)
-> Flexible timing (Daytime | Weekends)
-> Course Price: INR 45000
-> Location: SAHANA mehndi makeup nail studio academy

During the Qualified Makeup Artist program, you will learn advanced makeup techniques, including daily, evening, bridal, anti-ageing, catwalk, and glamour makeup. You will also learn how to create typology and character makeup looks, as well as how to perform eyebrow training, airbrush elite training, focus training, and stylization. This program provides you with the knowledge and skills to become a qualified makeup artist and receive an internationally recognized diploma.

Whether you're looking to work in the beauty industry or start your own makeup artistry business, our makeup training programs offer a comprehensive education that will help you achieve your career goals. With our training, you will be able to work in various industries, such as fashion, film, and television, or become self-employed as a makeup artist. Don't miss out on this opportunity to advance your skills and expand your career possibilities!
