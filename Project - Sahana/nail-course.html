U french

U-French nail art is a trendy and modern twist on the classic French manicure. It incorporates unconventional designs, vibrant colors, and creative embellishments, reflecting the youthful and expressive style of U-French culture. U-French nail art allows for personalized and unique nail designs that break away from traditional norms.

C french

C-French nail art, inspired by Canadian French culture, combines elegance and sophistication with a touch of artistic flair. It often incorporates iconic Canadian symbols, such as maple leaves or hockey motifs, into the designs. C-French nail art showcases a blend of traditional French nail aesthetics with a distinct Canadian twist.

V french

V-French nail art is a popular trend that adds a stylish and unique touch to the classic French manicure. It involves creating a V-shaped tip with contrasting colors or designs, giving nails a modern and eye-catching look. V-French nail art allows for endless creativity and personalization to showcase individual style.

Side french

Side French nail art is a trendy twist on the classic French manicure, where the white tip is positioned on the side of the nail instead of the traditional placement. This design adds a unique and edgy touch to your nails, making them stand out with a modern flair.

Basic nail art

Basic nail art involves simple and easy-to-create designs that are perfect for beginners. It typically includes techniques like using different colors, applying glitter, creating geometric patterns, or adding small decals or stickers to enhance the look of your nails. Basic nail art allows for creativity while keeping the designs clean and minimalist.

Metallic chrome

Metallic chrome nail art features a stunning mirror-like finish achieved by using metallic or chrome powder on the nails. This technique creates a high-shine and reflective effect that adds a glamorous and futuristic touch to your manicure. Metallic chrome nail art is a bold and attention-grabbing choice, perfect for making a statement with your nails.

Laser Chrome

Laser chrome nail art is a mesmerizing and futuristic style that incorporates holographic and iridescent effects on the nails. It involves using specialized laser chrome powders that create a multi-dimensional and shifting color effect, resembling the appearance of a laser beam. Laser chrome nail art is a high-impact design that catches the light and creates a stunning, eye-catching manicure.

Holographic chrome

Holographic chrome nail art is a captivating style that involves the application of holographic or iridescent pigments on the nails, creating a stunning rainbow effect that shifts and changes with the light. The holographic chrome finish adds a touch of magic and opulence to your nails, making them appear as if they're reflecting a spectrum of colors. Holographic chrome nail art is a popular choice for those seeking a dazzling and attention-grabbing manicure.

Chrome ombre

Chrome ombre nail art combines the trendy ombre technique with a sleek and metallic chrome finish. It involves blending two or more chrome nail polishes or powders together to create a gradient effect on the nails, transitioning from one color to another. This stylish and modern design adds a touch of sophistication and glamour to your manicure, making your nails shine with a seamless and eye-catching gradient.
