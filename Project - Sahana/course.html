Mehndi, Make-up & Nail Art
Welcome to Sahana Mehndi, Makeup, and Nail Art Academy!

Our academy is dedicated to helping aspiring artists achieve their dream job as an artist.

Being an artist is a unique profession that demands versatility, perfection, perseverance, courage, skill, and a keen eye for detail.

Our program is designed to cultivate and enhance these qualities, as we believe that professional success results from talent, diligence, and skill.

If you're passionate about the beauty industry and are ready to develop your craft, we're here to support you every step of the way.
