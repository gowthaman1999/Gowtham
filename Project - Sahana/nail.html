Poly Gel Extensions

> Poly Gel Extensions nail art is a type of nail enhancement technique that uses a gel-based substance called Poly Gel to extend the length of natural nails.

> Poly Gel is a hybrid between traditional gel and acrylic nail products, which allows it to be both flexible and strong.

> The Poly Gel is then cured under a UV or LED light to harden it into a durable and long-lasting extension.

> Poly Gel Extensions can last up to three weeks or more with proper care and maintenance.

Soft Gel Extensions

> Soft Gel Extensions nail art is a type of nail enhancement technique that uses a soft and flexible gel substance to extend the length of natural nails.

> To create Soft Gel Extensions, a nail technician applies a base coat to the natural nail and then applies layers of Soft Gel on top to build up the length and shape of the extension.

> Once the Soft Gel Extensions are in place, they can be filed and shaped just like natural nails and can be painted with regular nail polish or gel polish.

Gel & Acrylic Removal

> Gel and Acrylic Removal is the process of removing gel or acrylic nails from natural nails.

> To remove Gel or Acrylic nails, a nail technician will typically file the top layer of the nail to break the seal and then soak the nails in acetone.

> After soaking, the nail technician will use a tool to gently lift and scrape away the gel or acrylic.

> Once the Gel or Acrylic is removed, the natural nails are typically buffed and polished to remove any remaining residue and to smooth out the nail surface.
